== Installation ==

1. To Install Jakh 2015, you have to first install Twenty Fifteen WordPress Theme.
2. Navigate to **Appearance > Themes**  and click the **Add New** button.
2. Type **Twenty Fifteen** in the search form and press the **Enter** key on your keyboard.
4. Click the **Install** and then **Activate** button to begin using Twenty Fifteen Theme on your website.
5. Once Twenty Fifteen WordPress Theme is installed and activated, navigate again to **Appearance > Themes** 
and click the **Add New** button.
6. Type **Jakh** in the search form and press the **Enter** key on your keyboard.
7. Click the **Install** and then **Activate** button to begin using Jakh 20/15 on your website.
8. In your admin panel, navigate to **Appearance > Customize**.
9. Put the finishing touches on your website by adding a logo, header image, and custom colors.
